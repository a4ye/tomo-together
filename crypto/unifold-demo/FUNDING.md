# Funding

The **only** thing you fund is your **Unifold treasury account** with **USDC on Base**, via the Unifold dashboard.

**No gas wallet. No ETH. No faucets.** Unifold custodies the funds and pays the gas for every outbound transfer, so there is nothing else to top up.

## Fund the treasury

Make sure your Unifold treasury account (`ta_...`) holds **native Circle USDC on Base**:

```
0x833589fCD6eDb6E08f4c7C32D4f71b54bdA02913   (6 decimals)
```

- Each grant is **4 USDC** (`4000000` base units).
- **$4 in the treasury covers exactly one grant + one full cash-out.**
- Need more demo runs? **Top up USDC on Base.**

## Verifying

Check the treasury balance and outbound transfers in your **Unifold dashboard**.
